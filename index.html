THROWBACK THURSDAY

With shows that feel more like the house parties you threw while your parents were out of town, the band satisfies a long-ignored niche in Kentuckiana nightlife entertainment. Recalling the glory days of TRL, Warped Tour and MySpace, the band provides energetic renditions of songs from every millennial's awkward teenage years and pop-punk versions of today's Top 10. Throughout each show, the band keeps things fun and energetic, encouraging audience participation (inflatable guitar solo contests, etc.) to ensure the crowd stays on their feet from first round to last call.
